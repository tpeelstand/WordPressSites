What can go in the dumpster?

  • Drywall & Construcrtion/Remodeling Debris
  • Shingles
  • Scrap Wood
  • Carpeting
  • Old Furniture
  • Regular Household Trash
  • All that stuff you've been meaning to get rid of for years...

What can't go in the dumpster?

  • Appliances - Stoves, Refrigerators, Microwaves
  • TV's, monitors,Computers & Other Electronics
  • Recyclables, Batteries (auto, cycle, mower, ATV)
  • Concrete, Dirt, Yard Waste,Branches, Trees
  • Tires, Paint, Oil, Grease, Gasoline
  • Hazardous Materials - Asbestos, Chemicals
  • Liquids in Sealed Containers

How big are the dumpsters?

15 yard dumpsters are 8' wide x 14' long x 4' deep

20 yard dumpsters are 8' wide x 14' long x 5' deep.

A 15 yard dumpster will hold up to 55 square of shingles (which at about 5.5 tons of material, or 11,000 pounds).

We do not offer 20 yard dumpsters for roofing jobs.
